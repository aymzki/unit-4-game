Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a speific amount of points to your total score.

If your total score matches the random number, you win! If your total score goes over, you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game restarts, the values for each crystal will change.
